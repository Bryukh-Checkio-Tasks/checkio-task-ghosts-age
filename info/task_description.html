Nicola takes a moment to examine the ghosts. He is trying to think up a new method the determination just how old these ghosts are. He knows that age is related to their opacity. To the measure their opacity Nikola uses a scale of 10000 units to 0 units, where 10000 is a completely opaque newborn ghost (0 years in age) and 0 is completely transparent ghost (of an unknown age).

After some experimenting, Nikola discovered thinks he has discovered the law of ghostly opacity. On every birthday, a ghosts opacity is reduced by a number of units equal to its age when its age is a Fibonacci number (Read about this here) or increased by 1 if it is not.

For example:
A newborn ghost -- 10000 units of opacity.
1 year -- 10000 - 1 = 9999 (1 is a Fibonacci number).
2 year -- 9999 - 2 = 9997 (2 is a Fibonacci number).
3 year -- 9997 - 3 = 9994 (3 is a Fibonacci number).
4 year -- 9994 + 1 = 9995 (4 is not a Fibonacci number).
5 year -- 9995 - 5 = 9990 (1 is the Fibonacci number).

You should help Nicola to write a function which will determination the age of a ghost based on its opacity. You are given opacity measurements as a number ranging from 0 to 10000 inclusively. The ghosts cannot older than 5000 years as they simply disappear after such a long time.

This is a Halloween task so you should write a "magic" or "scary" solution. Please, do not write "regular" solution.

Input: An opacity measurement, an integer.

Output: The ghosts age, an integer.

checkio(9999) == 1
